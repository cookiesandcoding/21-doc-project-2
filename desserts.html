Tiramisu Recipe

  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup brewed espresso, cooled
  • 24-30 ladyfinger cookies
  • Cocoa powder for dusting
  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together mascarpone cheese, heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. Dip ladyfinger cookies into brewed espresso and layer them at the bottom of a serving dish.
  3. Spread a layer of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  4. Repeat layers of dipped ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture, finishing with a layer of mascarpone on top.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  6. Before serving, dust with cocoa powder.

New York Cheesecake Recipe

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press mixture onto the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in sour cream.
  5. Pour batter over crust in the pan. Smooth the top with a spatula.
  6. Bake for 55-60 minutes, or until center is almost set.
  7. Remove from oven and let cool on a wire rack for 10 minutes. Run a knife around the edges to loosen.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  9. Serve chilled. Optionally, top with fruit compote or whipped cream.

Enjoy these delicious desserts!
