Chocolate Banana Smoothie

  • 1 ripe banana, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup milk (any type)
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  1. Place all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes if desired for a colder smoothie.
  4. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

Avocado Toast

  • 2 slices whole grain bread, toasted
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (e.g., cilantro or parsley)
  1. Halve and pit the avocado, then scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
  3. Mash with a fork until smooth or chunky, as desired.
  4. Spread avocado mixture evenly onto toasted bread slices.
  5. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and fresh herbs.
  6. Serve immediately.

Caprese Skewers

  • Fresh mozzarella balls (bocconcini)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze or vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Thread mozzarella balls, cherry tomatoes, and basil leaves onto skewers.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze or vinegar.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Arrange on a serving platter and serve immediately.
