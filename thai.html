Pad Thai

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 100g tofu, cubed
  • 100g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • Lime wedges for serving
  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until softened. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add tofu and shrimp, stir-frying until shrimp turns pink and tofu is lightly browned.
  4. Push ingredients to the side of the pan, pour beaten eggs into the center, and scramble until cooked.
  5. Add rice noodles to the pan along with fish sauce, soy sauce, tamarind paste, and palm sugar. Toss to combine.
  6. Stir in bean sprouts and green onions, cooking until heated through.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with crushed peanuts and lime wedges.

Green Curry Chicken Recipe

  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 400g chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar
  • 10-12 Thai basil leaves
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving
  1. In a large pot or wok, heat half of the coconut milk over medium-high heat until it starts to bubble.
  2. Add green curry paste and stir-fry until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add chicken slices and cook until no longer pink.
  4. Pour in remaining coconut milk, bamboo shoots, and bell pepper. Stir well.
  5. Season with fish sauce and palm sugar, adjusting to taste.
  6. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until chicken is tender and flavors are well combined.
  7. Stir in Thai basil leaves just before serving.
  8. Serve hot with jasmine rice.

Tom Yum Goong Recipe

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, cut into 2-inch pieces and smashed
  • 4 kaffir lime leaves
  • 3 slices galangal or ginger
  • 2-3 Thai bird's eye chilies, crushed
  • 200g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup button mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
  1. In a pot, bring chicken or vegetable broth to a boil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal or ginger, and crushed chilies. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add shrimp, cherry tomatoes, and mushrooms. Cook until shrimp turns pink and opaque.
  4. Season with fish sauce, lime juice, and palm sugar. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  5. Remove from heat and discard lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal slices.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves.
