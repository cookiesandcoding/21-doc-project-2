• Cucumber Wrapped Salad

The Cucko Salad

  • 2 large cucumbers
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Using a vegetable peeler, peel the cucumbers lengthwise to create long strips (discard the first strip with seeds).
  2. In a bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, Kalamata olives, red onion, and basil leaves.
  3. Whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  4. Lay out cucumber strips flat. Spoon the salad mixture evenly onto each cucumber strip.
  5. Roll up the cucumber strips tightly, securing with toothpicks if needed.
  6. Arrange the cucumber wraps on a serving platter.
  7. Drizzle with remaining dressing and serve chilled.
