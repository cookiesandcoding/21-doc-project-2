Spaghetti Bolognese

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 300g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup beef or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, brown ground beef over medium-high heat.
  3. Add onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook until vegetables are softened.
  4. Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, broth, and red wine (if using).
  5. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  6. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti. Garnish with basil leaves and Parmesan cheese.

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

  • 300g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Add chicken slices and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  5. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  6. Stir in Parmesan cheese until sauce is smooth and thickened.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Toss cooked pasta with sauce until well coated.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Beef Stew

  • 500g stewing beef, cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add beef cubes and brown on all sides.
  3. Add onion, carrots, potatoes, and garlic. Sauté until vegetables are tender.
  4. Pour in beef broth and red wine (if using). Bring to a boil.
  5. Stir in diced tomatoes and dried thyme.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Cover and simmer over low heat for 1.5 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  8. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.
