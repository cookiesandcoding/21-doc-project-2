Butter Chicken

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  1. In a bowl, marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, and salt. Refrigerate for 2 hours.
  2. Heat butter in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  4. Pour in tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in cream and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Palak Paneer

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 bunches spinach, washed and chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  1. Blanch spinach in boiling water for 2 minutes. Drain and blend to a smooth paste.
  2. Heat ghee/oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions, green chili, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Stir in spinach puree, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add cream and paneer cubes. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Palak Paneer is ready to serve.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Chicken Biryani

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Whole spices: cloves, cardamom, cinnamon
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  1. Heat ghee/oil in a large pot. Add whole spices and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add sliced onions and cook until caramelized.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Cook until tomatoes are mushy.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  6. Stir in yogurt, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Layer soaked rice over the chicken mixture. Pour enough water to cover the rice.
  8. Cover and cook on low heat until rice and chicken are fully cooked.
  9. Fluff gently with a fork and serve hot.
